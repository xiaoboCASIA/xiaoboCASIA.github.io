About Me

Currently I am a Postdoctoral Scholar in Institute of Automation, Chinese Academic of Sciences.
2018~2020, I was a Researcher in JD AI Research, working with Prof. Tao Mei.
Before that, I received the PhD degree in National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition of Institute of Automation, Chinese Academic of Sciences , supervised by Prof. Stan Z. Li . I received the B.S degree in Computational Mathematics , Tianjin University.

My research interest mainly lies in Face Recognition, Model Compression, Object Detection and Image Classification.
